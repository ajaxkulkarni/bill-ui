1. Pay Per Bill Mobile Application

Why Pay Per Bill? 

Our app is a one stop interface for newspaper vendors and customers. It makes life easy when you want to pause delivery, pay bills online or subscribe to a new newspaper. 

Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, BHIM, UPI these payment methods are available with this application, customer can pay for their vendor with any of this payment method. The main objective of developing this application is to track daily newspaper quantity, daily orders, customer/vendor holidays, sms/email/WhatsApp alerts for payments from vendor to customer.

Online bill tracking and payment system is very important and helpful to reduce the petrol cost, travel cost and human efforts.

How to start?

This application is very easy and user friendly to use, to use this application android phone, internet connection is necessary and it should be available with good strength of network. This documentation will help you to use this application and also important to know more about it. Every vendor should read this document before using PayPerBill app on their mobile phone.

In this application vendor (vikreta) need to update their personal data like his full name, email, contact, PAN Id, Aadhar No, Business Name and Business license number after getting logged into this app with the photo copy of PAN id and Adhar No.

After updating and submitting personal information through this application PayPerBill will verify all those documents and then they will approve the vendor to use this application. (It will take approximately 1-2 working days to approve the vendor.)

Our PayPerBill application is available on Play Store after downloading that application vendor need to log in with own valid mobile number which will be used for login with OTP.

2. Screens & Application Functionality 

Login
1. Enter a valid mobile number 
2. Click on log in button
3. After that the verification screen will appear, OTP (sms) will auto fill verification code and move to the next screen. If OTP is not auto filled, wait for 2 minutes and enter the OTP manually and click on verify
4. If OTP is not received via SMS, click on RESEND to get OTP again.

Registration
1. After log in vendor needs to fill all this registration details to start using this application.
2. All fields are mandatory.
3. Vendor need to add PAN id image and Adhar No. image with number
4. In Business License vendor needs to add “Authorised Paper Vikreta License Number”
5. Vendor also needs to select all the locations where he delivers the newspaper.
6. After filling all the details click on save button to move to the next screen.

Bank Details
1. This screen is the default screen, home screen of this application.
2. On this screen vendor can see daily deliveries and no deliveries of newspapers.
3. Click on left side drawer button to fill the vendor bank information.
4. After fill all fields of bank details needs to click on save button, to move to the next screen
5. Bank details information is very important information to get payment in bank account directly.

Vendor Dashboard
1. After completing bank details vendor needs to add newspapers from vendor dashboard -> Manage Newspaper -> Add Newspaper for customer subscription
2. Add newspapers in application is one time task, it is helpful to add customers newspaper subscriptions.
3. After adding newspapers vendor needs to click on drawer tab to move to the next customer screen.

Customer List
1. After adding newspapers from the vendor dashboard, the next step is adding new Customer.
2. Vendor needs to click on Customer tab from the drawer.
3. Then need to click on add button to add new customer in system.
4. Vendor can fill the necessary information of customer and needs to click on save button to add that customer successfully.

Manage Subscriptions

1. This is customers profile screen, vendor can view and edit customers contact no., address, email from this screen.
2. He can directly call to the customer while using phone receiver logo.
3. After adding customers profile vendor need to add customers newspaper subscription for bill generation and monthly tracking.
4.Vendor can add scheme of newspaper while adding any particular newspaper like maharashtra times or lokmat and also vendor can add fixed monthly price for it
5. If customer wants to pause or stop subscription for particular newspaper vendor can do that from Subscribed Newspapers.
6. Vendor can pause a particular newspaper for customer if customer is on holiday or want to discontinue the particular newspaper service for some days
7.if customer wants to discontinue any news paper suspend option is available in customer subcribed newspapers menu, vendor can suspend ( delete ) a news paper from particular customers subcription

Bill Details
1. Vendor needs to add first bill for the customer from Bills Due tab.
2. Vendor needs to click on new button to create new bill for customer.
3. Vendor can create the bill as per the year, year drop down is available at Bill by year screen
4. Vendor needs to add newspaper quantity and amount details at the bottom of the billing screen.
5. Bill amount, service charge, pending amount, credit amount all fields are editable, vendor can change those amounts.
6. After filling all the details vendor needs to click on save changes button.
7. Then click on send button to send that bill to customer through SMS, WhatsApp, E-mail.

Invoices and Orders
1. In this order tab vendor can see daily pick up orders which he is going to deliver to customers in future we are adding cost price also to pay hassle free amount to their respective distributors
2. In this invoices tab vendor can see all the pending bill of customers and he can search and also remind them all to pay their bills in one click
